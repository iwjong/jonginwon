As a UX/UI Designer, I have extensive experience in designing graphical user interfaces for feature phones catering to the European and Asian mobile markets. I have worked with major operators such as Orange and Vodafone in Europe and NTT Docomo in Japan. My expertise in understanding the unique research, business, and technical requirements of each market, along with my design sensibility, has allowed me to create compelling products that stand out in the market. I was responsible for leading the visual aspect of the project, from defining the visual style to the interaction design, to ensure that the final product met the highest standards. I also managed the creation of artworks and provided visual support throughout the entire project, from prototype to launch. In addition, I played a crucial role in improving the structure of the information architecture of the feature phones to make the user experience more intuitive and enjoyable.
