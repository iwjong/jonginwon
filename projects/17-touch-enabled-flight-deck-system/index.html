The focus of my work was to gain a comprehensive understanding of the existing synoptic pages and the various controls that needed to be integrated into the new touch screen synoptic system. I took the initiative to create wireframes that effectively illustrate the ideas and concepts I developed. I also created high-fidelity prototypes to showcase the functional improvements made to the user interface.

Throughout the project, I dedicated a significant amount of time and effort towards comprehending the layout of the existing synoptic system and identifying areas for improvement. This task was quite challenging as I had to determine the best way to seamlessly incorporate a vast array of physical controls into the digital interface while ensuring that the interaction patterns were consistent with those that pilots are accustomed to when flying.
