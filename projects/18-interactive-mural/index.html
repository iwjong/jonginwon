A digital wall art was created that incorporated a unique space with an interconnected design, featuring interactive activities that encouraged engagement and exploration. Passersby were able to interact with the environment using motion and external audio detection, causing the landscape to change and the particles to dance on the mapped walls. The goal was to make the indoor space an extension of the surrounding area, encouraging people to connect with the environment in a playful and dynamic way. With the use of cutting-edge technology, the digital playground provided a dynamic and engaging experience that stimulated the senses and encouraged people to explore and interact with the environment. Whether it was through the movement of their bodies or the sounds they made, the experience was designed to be immersive and engaging, encouraging people to explore the space and be a part of the digital playground.
