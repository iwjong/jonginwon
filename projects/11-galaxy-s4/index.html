As a UI Designer on the Advanced UX Design Unit, I was involved in the creation of cutting-edge user experiences for the lock/home screen and application interface/interaction design of the Galaxy S4. Along with my team, I was responsible not only for designing existing features but also for conceptualizing and developing new ideas for future releases.

Our team's efforts were reflected in the latest release of Android 4.2.2 and Samsung's Touchwiz UX, which included several of our innovative designs. One such concept was the "Glance View" feature, which transformed the way users interact with their device. By displaying recommended and relevant content at the top of the screen, this feature allowed users to easily access and interact with various content simply by touching the top of the screen. This groundbreaking design received widespread recognition and helped to establish the Galaxy S4 as a leading smartphone in the market.
