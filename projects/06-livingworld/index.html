In this project, I played a pivotal role in the development of uGo, a new adaptive user interface that was created through a collaborative effort between Samsung and Adobe. I led envisioning and bringing to life a main screen that dynamically displayed a landmark picture of the user's location, complete with playful graphics and animations that were adjusted in real-time to reflect the time of day or night.

To effectively alert users to missed calls and other important information, I leveraged my Adobe AIR expertise to design engaging animations that caught attention while keeping the experience seamless and enjoyable. My responsibilities extended beyond design and included driving the project from concept to completion, developing solutions that met requirements and objectives, and providing ongoing support to software engineers throughout the product development cycle.

Through my contributions to the uGo project, I worked closely with cross-functional teams, managed multiple tasks and timelines, and brought innovative design solutions to life. The final product delivered a clearer, more usable experience and established a disciplined process for moving adaptive UI concepts into production.
