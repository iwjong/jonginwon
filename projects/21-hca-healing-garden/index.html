The outdoor healing garden and play space at Studer Family Children's Hospital is a place of respite and rejuvenation for patients, families, and clinical team members. With its inviting design, this outdoor area provides an opportunity for those who spend time in the hospital to connect with nature and engage in playful activities.

Inspired by the rich history and stunning natural beauty of Savannah, the outdoor healing garden and play space is designed to embody the essence of the outdoors. The space features lush greenery, soothing water elements, and playful structures that encourage exploration and interaction. The simplicity of the design is intentional, inviting patients and families to step away from the stress and worries of a hospital setting and find solace in the peace and calm of nature.

The aim of the outdoor healing garden and play space is to provide a place where patients and families can spend time and heal in a peaceful, natural environment. This space offers a therapeutic escape from the clinical aspects of hospital life, providing a much-needed break from the distractions and stress of hospitalization. The outdoor healing garden and play space is also a place of rejuvenation for the clinical team members, who often spend long hours providing care to patients.

At Studer Family Children's Hospital, we believe that access to nature and playful activities are essential components of the healing process. The outdoor healing garden and play space is designed to bring these elements to life, providing a refreshing and rejuvenating experience for all who visit.
