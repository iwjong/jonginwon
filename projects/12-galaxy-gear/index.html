As a member of the Advanced UX Team, I was responsible for designing interfaces and prototypes for the initial concept of a wearable device. Working within the constraints of the Tizen platform, I created a streamlined interface that allowed users to easily access a variety of the device's functions with a simple double tap on the home screen.

One of the key design elements I implemented was the double tap feature, which could be customized according to the user's preferences. This feature provided immediate access to functions such as adjusting sound volume and screen brightness, eliminating the need for a physical home key. The success of this design led to the registration of a patent for the double tap feature, making it a unique and innovative solution for wearable devices.

The focus on simplicity and ease of use in my designs helped to set this wearable device apart from others in the market and establish it as a leader in the field.
