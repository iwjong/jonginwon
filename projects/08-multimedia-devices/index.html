I have had the privilege of working with renowned companies such as Adidas, Adobe, Giorgio Armani, Blurb, and Bang & Olufsen. My experience in collaborating with diverse markets has enabled me to bring a unique perspective to each project, combining the company's brand philosophy with new design solutions.

One of my notable projects was with Adidas, where I led conceptualizing and designing the user experience for the myCoach platform. This project had the potential to shape the company's digital footprint and expand its market reach. I applied my expertise in visual and interaction design to create a seamless user experience aligned with the Adidas brand identity.

Another project I was involved in was the development of multimedia services for music devices. My role included contributing to the interface consistency of F-series feature phones, which were designed for young customers in the global market. I initiated the interface design principles for these devices, focusing on a multimedia-based approach that has remained central to my work since 2006 and improved clarity and usability across the product line.
