As a critical member of the design team, I played a significant role in the development of Touchwiz 3.0, the pioneering smartphone that introduced the Samsung Bada Operating System (WAVE) platform. Working closely with a team of four other talented designers, we delivered an advanced user interface that set the standard for future Samsung devices.

As the lead designer for Touchwiz UX 3.0, I was responsible for bringing the visual and interaction design to life for the Galaxy S1 to S8 series and the Galaxy Note series. I leveraged my expertise in user-centered design to create a visually appealing and intuitive experience for users, with a focus on empowering them to easily search, organize, and manage their apps.

With the release of the Galaxy S3, I took my design efforts to the next level by further refining the user experience. I enhanced the UI navigation, editing mode, and widget services to make it even more intuitive and user-friendly. The result was a more fluid and productive user experience that met the needs and expectations of the target audience.

My work on Touchwiz 3.0 showcased my exceptional design skills, creativity, and ability to drive results. It has earned me recognition within the industry and solidified my reputation as a talented and results-driven UI/Interaction Designer. I am proud to have been a key contributor to the development of Touchwiz 3.0, a groundbreaking project that has had a lasting impact on the smartphone industry.
