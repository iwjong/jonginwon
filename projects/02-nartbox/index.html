With over two decades of experience using Adobe Flash and ActionScript, I have honed my skills in creating a wide range of digital products, from flash games to mobile home screens. My focus has always been on creating an uncluttered look, smooth animations, and intuitive interaction, all of which are essential components of an enjoyable user experience.

One of the proudest moments in my career as a Flash developer came when a friend of mine shared with me that he had spent half a morning playing a flash game that I had created with his 6-year-old daughter. The fact that my work had not only captured the attention of a young child but also fostered creativity and social interaction was incredibly rewarding. This experience further reinforced my belief in the power of simple, yet engaging digital products to foster imagination, creativity, and connection.
