This collaboration with Telfair Children's Art Museum focused on making foundational art ideas more accessible to younger audiences. The project aimed to connect historical movements, including Impressionist references, with contemporary digital experiences for children and families.

The design approach combined visual storytelling with a flexible identity system built around geometric forms, color relationships, and layered compositions. Drawing from art education frameworks and museum goals, we developed a brand language that could support engagement across physical and digital touchpoints.

The result was a contemporary, adaptable museum identity that supports early art learning while remaining clear and inviting for families. The system created a practical foundation for future exhibits and programs that blend education, storytelling, and interaction.
