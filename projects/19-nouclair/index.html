Nouclair was developed in Savannah as a cosmetics brand centered on sustainable and ethical practice. The project needed a brand voice and presentation that made its emphasis on raw, natural ingredients clear from the start.

Around this time, I was moving from single-screen decisions toward clearer interaction systems developed with cross-functional teams. The direction focused on consistency across products and services, with messaging that reinforced ingredient transparency and avoided the language of synthetic-heavy beauty positioning. Every touchpoint was shaped to communicate care, clarity, and a more responsible approach to personal care.

The result is a brand identity that aligns product philosophy with customer expectations around quality and integrity. Nouclair presents natural ingredient values as a practical standard rather than a marketing add-on. The outcome moved the work forward, and the unresolved details became useful lessons for later projects.
